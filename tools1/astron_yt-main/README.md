### ASTRON

YouTube videos without ads, downloads 4K quality videos, and supports background playback.

◈ Key Features ◈

• **Ad-free Experience**: Watch videos immediately without any advertising.

• **High-Quality Downloads**: Download videos in up to 4K quality and MP3 formats for offline viewing.

• **Background Playback**: Keep the video playing while using other apps or with the screen turned off.

• **Notification Section**: Stay updated with a dedicated notification section, just like the official Android YouTube app.

• **Playlist Management**: Easily add any video to your playlists with options available for every video.

• **Proper Subtitles**: Enhanced support for subtitles to ensure a better viewing experience.

• **Bypass Quality Restrictions**: Access the highest available quality for all videos without restrictions.

• **Audio Language Selection**: Choose your preferred audio language for supported videos.

• **User-Friendly Interface**: We collect and reflect user feedback to continuously improve the user experience.

• **Device Compatibility**
App available for Android. This app works with Android 7.0 or higher.
